Privacy Policy

Jamie's Food Tracker respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how it may be shared when you use Jamie's Food Tracker.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, and login details.
  • Food log information, including meal names, descriptions, estimated calories, macros, meal times, and revision history.
  • Photos you choose to submit for meal or leftover estimates.
  • App usage information, such as estimate requests, credit balance, transaction history, and error logs.
  • Device and technical information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app.

How We Use Information

We use this information to:

  • Create and manage your app account.
  • Estimate calories, protein, carbs, fat, and other nutrition information.
  • Save your food history and meal revisions.
  • Manage app credits, purchases, and estimate usage.
  • Provide support and troubleshoot issues.
  • Improve app reliability, safety, and user experience.

AI Meal Estimates

Jamie's Food Tracker may send meal descriptions, food photos, leftover photos, and related meal context to AI service providers to generate nutrition estimates. These estimates are approximate and may be inaccurate.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party service providers to operate the app, including:

  • Supabase, for authentication, database, backend functions, and storage.
  • OpenAI, for AI-based food and nutrition estimation.
  • Google Play, for app distribution and payments.
  • Payment processors or billing providers, if purchases are enabled.

These providers may process data as needed to provide their services.

Photos

Photos submitted for meal estimates may be processed by our backend and AI service providers. Recent thumbnails may be stored on your device. Server-side photo handling may change as the app develops, but photos will only be used to provide app functionality, improve estimates, support the user experience, or comply with legal obligations.

Payments and Credits

Jamie's Food Tracker may offer paid downloads, starter estimate credit, or in-app purchases. We track app credit balances and estimate usage to provide paid services and prevent abuse. App credits have no cash value, are not financial products, and cannot be withdrawn, transferred, or redeemed outside the app.

Data Retention

We keep account, food log, estimate, credit, and support data for as long as needed to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and improve our services. You may request deletion of your account or data by contacting support.

Your Choices

You may choose not to submit meal photos or food descriptions. Some features may not work without this information. You may request account deletion or data access by contacting us.

Children

Jamie's Food Tracker is not intended for children under 13 and is not designed for use by children or families as defined by Google Play's Families policies.

Health Disclaimer

Jamie's Food Tracker is for general wellness and food tracking. It is not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or a substitute for professional nutrition, medical, or healthcare advice.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect user information. No system is perfectly secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact: support@jamiesfoodtracker.com
