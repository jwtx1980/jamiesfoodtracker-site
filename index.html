Jamie's Food Tracker

A simpler way to log meals, estimate calories, and keep track of macros.

Jamie's Food Tracker helps users log food with text, photos, and manual entries. The app can estimate calories, protein, carbs, and fat, then lets users revise estimates or adjust meals with leftover photos.

Available for private beta testing on Android.
