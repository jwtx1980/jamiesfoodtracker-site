Delete Account

You can request deletion of your Jamie's Food Tracker account and app data by contacting support.

How to Request Deletion

Email support@jamiesfoodtracker.com with the subject "Data deletion request." Please send the request from the email address used for your app account when possible.

What to Include

  • The email address used for your app account.
  • A short note confirming that you want your account deleted.
  • Any details that may help us find the correct account.

What May Be Deleted

Account information, food logs, estimates, revisions, credit history, uploaded meal photos, and support data associated with your account may be deleted, subject to legal, security, fraud prevention, payment, and operational retention requirements.

After Deletion

Some records may be retained when required for legal compliance, dispute resolution, security, abuse prevention, accounting, or app store purchase records. Deleted data may not be recoverable.
